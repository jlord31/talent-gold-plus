About Us

Talent gold plus is digital sport marketplace that connect investors to choose a raw but talented youngster, including women in football and athletics, whom they can invest in with the option to gain a return on their investment once the athlete signs a professional contract – much the same manner as more traditional seed investors.

A safe, supportive and secure environment, helping nurture, mentor young talents and providing them with all the physical, emotional, mental and psychological support they need to be able to succeed.

Support and nurture potential talent from the community, Giving investors the Opportunity to buy into and support the talent’s development whether for profit, or to give back.

Get Involved

Talent

We actively identify and train sports talents from grassroots to elite levels in the UK, guiding them to become professionals up to Olympic standards. You are invited to create your profiles and bios to showcase your talents with video clips.

Investors

Become a member of our vibrant community. Whether you are a seasoned investor or a philanthropist seeking to make a meaningful impact, support or sponsor our sports talents. Explore our diverse portfolio: Uncover the exceptional abilities.

Partner

Your collaboration has the potential to reshape individual lives, families, and communities, thereby contributing to the mitigation of social inequality. You can make a difference through contributions, donations, corporate volunteering, provision of training spaces, sports equipment, sponsorship, or fundraising efforts.

Professionals

We partner with carefully selected world-class coaches, mentors, sports agents, Phycologist, massage therapist and other sports professionals to provide their services on our sports platform connecting them with our talents and users.

Ambassadors

At Talent Gold Plus, we believe in using sports to create positive change in the community. Our esteemed ambassadors are not only distinguished athletes but also fervent advocates of leadership principles and values.

Volunteer

If you enjoy collaborating with athletes, technophiles, and social media influencers, and are willing to share your expertise, this opportunity is tailor-made for you. The commitment required is a minimum of 3 hours per week, with occasional weekend involvement.

Train with us

We go beyond physical training because peak performance requires both a strong mind and a strong body. Our ‘Performance Training’ program is designed to unlock your athletic potential through mental conditioning.

Learn with Us

Youths and students often lack opportunities to cultivate soft skills in schools due to the absence of platforms for self and peer-to-peer leadership exercises. We intend to change this to enhance their academic progress.

Sponsors & Donors

We believe in the transformative power of sports to shape character and foster community, and our athletes and teams embody this spirit of excellence. We welcome donations in various forms—money, sports equipment, training time/facilities, kits, and therapies—to help us make a difference in the community through sports.

Meet Our Olympians

Chioma Ajunwa

First African woman to win an Olympic gold medal in a field event and is the only person to transition from football to athletics and secure an Olympic gold medal.

Dr. Micheal Bruce

Cognitive Psychologist, Elite Speed & Performance coach specializing in Premiership football. Former England International Athlete and Olympic Gold Medal-winning coach.

Ruth Balofin

Bronze medals in snatch, clean and jerk, and overall at the 1999 World Championships (75 kg). Silver medal at the 2000 Summer Olympics (75 kg) with a total lift of 245.0 kg.

Glory Alozie

Competed in hurdling for Nigeria and Spain. she was World junior second placer in 1996 and later became African champion twice, holding records in 100m hurdles.

Stepani Douglas

Silver medal winnner in 4 x 100m relay at 1990 Commonwealth Games, New Zealand, and bronze medal winnner at 1994 Commonwealth Games, Canada.

Marcus Adam

Gold medalist in the 200m and 4x100m relay at 1990 Commonwealth Games, also won silver in 4x100m relay at 1990 European Championships.
